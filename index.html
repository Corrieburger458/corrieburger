About us

Academic Achievement Centre is located in Mokopane/Potgietersrus. Our Truter Centre is a registered Impaq-supported learning facility offering comprehensive academic assistance to homeschooling families. We specialise in providing structured daily routines, tutoring support, supervision, and resource guidance aligned with the Impaq curriculum. Our centre is committed to fostering academic excellence, confidence, and independent learning in every student. In addition, we are fully registered as an examination centre, ensuring that learners can complete their assessments and exams in a safe, professional, and compliant environment. We focus on strong learning, discipline, and one-on-one attention. Our goal is to build a bright future.
